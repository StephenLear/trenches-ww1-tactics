# Privacy Policy for Trenches: WW1 Tactics

**Last Updated: February 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Trenches: WW1 Tactics ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Summary

**We do not collect any personal data.** Trenches: WW1 Tactics is designed to be played entirely offline without requiring any personal information from you.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.
- **No Location Data**: We do not access or track your location.
- **No Device Information**: We do not collect information about your device, operating system, or hardware.
- **No Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you play the game, what features you use, or how long you play.
- **No Advertising Data**: We do not collect data for advertising purposes or share data with advertisers.
- **No Third-Party Tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All game data is stored locally on your device:

- **Game Progress**: Your campaign progress, completed missions, and unlocked achievements are saved on your device only.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (sound, music, language, difficulty) are stored locally.
- **Veteran Soldiers**: Your soldier roster and their progression are stored on your device.

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by us
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

### No Account Required

Trenches: WW1 Tactics does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any credentials to play.

## No Internet Connection Required

The Game is fully playable offline. No internet connection is required or used during gameplay. The Game does not make any network requests.

## Children's Privacy

Trenches: WW1 Tactics does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The Game is rated 12+ for mild realistic violence related to its World War I historical theme.

## Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services that would collect your data:

- No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No crash reporting services that collect personal data

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to:
- Access
- Correct
- Delete
- Export

Your game data exists only on your device and is under your complete control.

## Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and we do not transmit any information:
- There is no risk of your data being intercepted during transmission
- There is no central database that could be breached
- Your gaming experience remains completely private

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. Please check this policy periodically for updates.
